Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How many times can it charge an iPhone 14?

The Anker 20000mAh Power Bank can charge an iPhone 14 approximately 4-5 times on a single charge.

Does it support fast charging for all devices?

It supports fast charging for devices compatible with Anker's PowerIQ and VoltageBoost technologies, which covers most modern smartphones and tablets.

Can I charge my laptop with this power bank?

This specific model is designed primarily for smartphones and tablets. While it might provide a partial charge to some smaller USB-C laptops, it's not optimized for laptop charging.

How long does it take to fully recharge the power bank?

Recharging the 20000mAh capacity can take around 9-10 hours with a standard 2A charger. Using a faster charger can reduce this time.
