How long does it take to fully recharge the Anker 737?

When recharged with a 140W USB-C PD 3.1 compatible wall charger (not included), the Anker 737 can be fully recharged in approximately 50-60 minutes. Slower chargers will take proportionally longer.

Is the Anker 737 Power Bank safe to use?

Yes, Anker products are known for their safety features. The Anker 737 includes Anker's ActiveShield 2.0 technology, which monitors temperature over 3 million times a day to protect against overheating and overcharging, ensuring safety for both the power bank and your devices.

Can I take the Anker 737 on an airplane?

With a capacity of 24,000mAh (90Wh), the Anker 737 Power Bank is generally within the TSA/IATA limits for carry-on luggage (typically 100Wh). Always check with your specific airline's regulations before flying, but it should be permitted in carry-on bags.
