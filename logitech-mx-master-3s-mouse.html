8K DPI Darkfield Sensor

Quiet Click Buttons

Enjoy a satisfying click with 90% less noise than previous MX Master models, allowing for focused work without distraction.

Ergonomic Sculpted Design

Customizable Buttons & Thumb Wheel

Assign custom functions to buttons and the thumb wheel using Logitech Options+ software for personalized workflows.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes the MX Master 3S different from the MX Master 3?

The MX Master 3S features quieter clicks (90% less noise) and an upgraded 8000 DPI sensor compared to the MX Master 3's 4000 DPI sensor.

Can I use the MX Master 3S on any surface?

Yes, the 8K DPI Darkfield sensor allows you to track accurately on virtually any surface, including glass.

How many devices can I connect to the MX Master 3S?

You can connect to up to three devices simultaneously via Bluetooth Low Energy or the Logi Bolt USB receiver.

What is Logitech Flow?

Logitech Flow is a software feature that allows you to seamlessly control multiple computers with a single MX Master 3S mouse, including copy-pasting text, images, and files between them.

How long does the battery last?

A full charge provides up to 70 days of use. A quick 3-minute charge can give you up to 3 hours of use.
