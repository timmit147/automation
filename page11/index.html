Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How much does the Razer Viper V3 HyperSpeed weigh?

The Razer Viper V3 HyperSpeed weighs approximately 82 grams with a standard AA battery inserted, making it one of the lightest wireless gaming mice available.

Does the Razer Viper V3 HyperSpeed use a rechargeable battery?

No, the Viper V3 HyperSpeed operates on a single AA battery, which provides an impressive battery life of up to 280 hours.

What sensor is used in the Razer Viper V3 HyperSpeed?

No, the Razer Viper V3 HyperSpeed focuses purely on performance and weight reduction, thus it does not feature any RGB lighting.
