Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can I use the Galaxy Buds2 Pro with non-Samsung phones?

Yes, you can use them with any Bluetooth-enabled device, including iPhones and Android phones. However, you'll miss out on some advanced features like Auto Switch and 360 Audio customization, which require the Samsung Wearable app and a Samsung device.

How good is the Active Noise Cancellation?

The ANC on the Galaxy Buds2 Pro is among the best in its class, effectively reducing ambient noise like airplane engines, traffic, and office chatter. It's adaptive, meaning it can adjust its intensity based on your surroundings.

Are they comfortable for long listening sessions?

Yes, the ergonomic design and lightweight build are specifically engineered for comfort. Many users find them comfortable for hours on end.

What is 24-bit Hi-Fi audio?

It refers to a higher resolution audio format that offers more detail and dynamic range than standard CD-quality audio (16-bit). This results in a richer, more immersive listening experience, provided you have compatible source audio.
