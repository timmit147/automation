Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the battery life of the Sony WH-1000XM5? +
The Sony WH-1000XM5 offers up to 30 hours of playback with Active Noise Cancellation enabled, and up to 40 hours with ANC turned off.
Can I use these headphones with multiple devices at once? +
Yes, the WH-1000XM5 supports multipoint connection, allowing you to connect to two Bluetooth devices simultaneously.
Are the Sony WH-1000XM5 waterproof? +
No, the Sony WH-1000XM5 are not waterproof. They are designed for listening and should be kept away from excessive moisture.
Does it come with a 3.5mm audio jack? +
The Sony WH-1000XM5 does not have a built-in 3.5mm audio jack. You will need a USB-C to 3.5mm audio cable or adapter for wired listening.
