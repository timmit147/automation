Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes the OmniPoint switches so special?
The OmniPoint switches are Hall Effect magnetic switches that allow you to adjust the actuation distance for each key individually, from a lightning-fast 0.4mm to a more standard 3.6mm. This means you can tailor the responsiveness to your exact preference for gaming or typing.
Is the OLED Smart Display useful?
Yes, the OLED display is surprisingly useful. It can show game information, Discord notifications, media playback controls, and even custom logos, providing quick access to information without alt-tabbing.
Is the wrist rest comfortable?
The included magnetic wrist rest is well-padded and ergonomically designed, offering significant comfort during extended gaming or typing sessions. It attaches securely with magnets.
Can I use this keyboard for both gaming and typing?
Absolutely. The adjustable actuation allows you to set keys for ultra-fast gaming responses or a more deliberate typing feel. Many users find it excels at both.
