Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms is the Arctis Nova 7 Wireless compatible with?

The Arctis Nova 7 Wireless is highly versatile, compatible with PC, PlayStation (PS4/PS5), Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices via its compact USB-C wireless dongle. It also supports Bluetooth for additional connectivity options.

How long does the battery last on a single charge?

You can expect up to 38 hours of battery life on a single charge, ensuring your headset keeps up with even the longest gaming marathons. It also features USB-C fast charging, providing 6 hours of use from just a 15-minute charge.

Can I use the Arctis Nova 7 Wireless in a wired mode?

Yes, the headset includes a 3.5mm audio cable, allowing you to use it in wired mode if you prefer, or if the battery runs out. This ensures maximum flexibility for all your audio needs.

Is the microphone detachable or retractable?

The ClearCast Gen 2 microphone is fully retractable into the earcup, providing a sleek look when not in use. It's not detachable, but its discreet design makes it virtually invisible when tucked away.

What's the main difference between the Nova 7 and Nova Pro?

While both are premium headsets, the Nova Pro series typically offers even higher-end features like a GameDAC, hot-swappable batteries, and often slightly superior audio drivers or active noise cancellation. The Nova 7 provides an excellent balance of features, performance, and value without reaching the Pro's ultimate premium tier.
